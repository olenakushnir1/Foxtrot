Frequently asked

Questions

HOW DOES FOXTROT WORK

When using Foxtrot to build your campaign, simply register, purchase your plan and start building your campaign with an easy to use drag and drop builder. Install Foxtrot onto your website with a code injection or an overlay plug-in. When using Foxtrot once your campaign is completed, find the Foxtrot button on your site, open your camera, scan an object/screen which will lead you to your campaign page.

WHAT’S ALL THE POSSIBILITIES?

Foxtrot enables you to define your campaigns with unlimited design options. The limit of your AR power depends on which package you choose and how you choose to create your campaign.

WHAT ARE MY PAYMENT OPTIONS?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and (in select countries) direct debit/wire payment. We also support transactions in a range of currencies.

RESTRICTIONS?

In order to provide you with secure data and personal information, it’s a requirement that you have an SSL certificate on your website to register and purchase a Foxtrot package.

HOW TO PURCHASE?

Sign up through the website using your email address, enter the OPT (One Time Pin) you were emailed, enter your details to register your account, choose your package and payment method, and now you can start building your AR campaign.

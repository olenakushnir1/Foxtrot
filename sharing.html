foxtrot tips

Once you’ve created your campaign, Foxtrot will give you an overplayed plug-in or a code to copy and paste into your website’s code.

To catch the eye of your customers use short, punchy videos no longer that a minute and a half. Use high res JPEGs and PNGs to make your campaign pop and keep your text short and to the point of your campaign.

For images, JPEGS and PNGS are the only formats allowed to be used for Foxtrot. While when using video, only mp4 files are allowed to be used.

If you have an internal dev team, you can customise you foxtrot experience for your users.
